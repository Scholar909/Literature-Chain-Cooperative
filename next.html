Literature Chain Cooperative – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 02 July 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect one piece of personal information: the verified Google email address you choose when signing in to post a review.

2. How We Use Information

Your email is stored with your review to let you edit or delete your own comment and to prevent impersonation. It is not used for marketing or shared with third parties.

3. Cookies & Analytics

Literature Chain does not set tracking cookies and does not use analytics or advertising networks.

4. Third-Party Purchase Links

When you click “Buy” you leave our site and visit a seller’s page. Any data you provide there is governed by the seller’s privacy policy.

5. User-Generated Content

Reviews you publish are public. Please do not include sensitive personal information in your comments.

6. Data Security

Reviews and email addresses are stored in Google Firestore, which encrypts data in transit and at rest. Only you can remove your own review; staff access is restricted by Firebase security rules.

7. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we learn that a child’s email has been collected, we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

You may delete your review (and thus your stored email) at any time using the trash-can icon next to your comment.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date will change accordingly, and continued use constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.

10. Contact Us

For privacy questions, email admin@literaturechain.com.
